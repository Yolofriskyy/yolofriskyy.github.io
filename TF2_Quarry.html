TF2 'Quarry'

King Of The Hill map developed using Valve's Hammer engine

Walkthrough video

Blockout vs Final product

When comparing the blockout to the final product, several additional refinements were made. Beyond texturing and meshing, lighting was positioned throughout the map to illuminate previously dark areas. The global lighting was adjusted to reduce exposure and overall brightness, and a yellow tint was added to highlight the sandy and rocky regions of the map.

Most conflict points on the map were meticulously developed, providing players with multiple paths and flanks to effectively capture the objective. The addition of meshing enhanced cover for both teams and minimized sightlines. Although the map's gameplay and conflict points were well-designed, there was room for further visual enhancements. Detailing the rock walls, adding rocks and a roof to the cave and incorporating scenery along the outer ridges, such as construction areas with vehicles. Additionally, adding more detail to each building's exterior and interior, like wooden beams, windows, and props, would've improved the overall visual appeal. Now that I no longer have a time constraint, I can go back to this project and further improve it in the future.
