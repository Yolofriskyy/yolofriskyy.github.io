Harry Simmons

Game Developer | Programmer | Designer

I’m Harry, a UK-based game developer with a passion for creating unique and engaging gameplay experiences. I graduated with a 2:1 Honours Bsc in Computer Games Design and Programming from Staffordshire University.

I tend to hyper-focus on solving problems that catch my interest, always pushing myself to generate new ideas while constantly learning and improving with every project I take on. I thrive in team environments, where collaboration allows me to deliver the best possible work within each given timeframe.

Multiplayer games are my favourite sub-genre, I love the fun and interaction they bring when playing with friends. I’m versed in developing multiplayer experiences, and many of my projects revolve around that passion.

This portfolio is my way of showcasing upcoming projects and ideas interactively. If you're interested in my work or want to chat, feel free to reach out on GitHub, LinkedIn or by leaving a comment directly on my portfolio!
